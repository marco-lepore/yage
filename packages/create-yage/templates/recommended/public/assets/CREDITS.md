# Asset credits

The assets in this directory were included to give new projects something
visible out of the box. Replace them with your own.

## Player sprites

- `player-idle.png` — 10-frame idle strip (480×48, 48×48 per frame)
- `player-walk.png` — 8-frame walk strip (384×48)
- `player-jump.png` — 3-frame jump strip (144×48)

By [Zegley](https://zegley.itch.io/2d-platformermetroidvania-asset-pack).

## Coin, slime, and sound effects

- `coin.png` — 12-frame spin strip (192×16, 16×16 per frame)
- `slime_purple.png` — 4×3 grid (96×72, 24×24 per frame)
- `jump.wav` — jump sound
- `hurt.wav` — hurt sound

From the [Brackeys Platformer Bundle](https://brackeysgames.itch.io/brackeys-platformer-bundle).
